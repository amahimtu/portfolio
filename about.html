Hi, I’m a UX and product designer. I leverage principles and concepts from human-computer interaction (HCI) and marketing to design intuitive experiences and products that are useful, usable, and desirable.

Before transitioning into design, I worked in the marketing divisions of world-renowned companies in the audio and music industries. My UX skills, coupled with my marketing background, have given me a holistic understanding of the design process within the larger business context.

I earned a Master of Information degree from the University of Toronto and a Bachelor of Arts (with distinction) from the University of Michigan, double majoring in communication & media, and music.

As a musician, I am passionate about music technology and performance, drums and percussion, and all things audio. Design and music aside, I enjoy hiking, reading philosophy, playing soccer, hanging out with my dogs, travelling around the world, and coffee. Lots of coffee.
