Hi, I’m a UX and product designer. I leverage concepts from human-computer interaction (HCI) and marketing to design useful, usable, and desirable experiences and products.

Before transitioning into design, I worked in the marketing divisions of world-renowned pro audio and music companies. My UX skills, coupled with my marketing background, allow me to balance execptional experience design with cross-functional business objectives.

I hold a Master of Information degree from the University of Toronto and a Bachelor of Arts from the University of Michigan.

As a musician, I am passionate about music technology and performance, drums and percussion, and all things audio. Design and music aside, I enjoy hiking, reading philosophy, playing soccer, hanging out with my dogs, spontaneous trips, and coffee. Lots of coffee.
